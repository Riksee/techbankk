About Techbankk

Techbankk International Ltd is a forward-thinking technology firm founded in 2023 by a group of educators and passionate tech enthusiasts. We are committed to using technology to enhance and transform learning experiences in the digital era.

Based in Lagos, Techbankk has emerged as a pioneer in educational technology, rising to meet the increasing demand for innovative learning solutions. Our company is organized into specialized departments that work collaboratively to deliver cutting-edge services:

  • Web Development Department
  • UI/UX Design Department
  • Embedded Systems Department

Our Mission

Techbankk is dedicated to designing and delivering technology-driven educational programs that empower tech enthusiasts to thrive in today’s digital world.

Our Vision

We envision a future where quality tech education is accessible to everyone and lifelong learning opportunities are encouraged and celebrated.
